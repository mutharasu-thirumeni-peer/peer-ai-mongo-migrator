This diagram shows the intelligent consolidation of 22 PostgreSQL tables into 17 optimized MongoDB collections. The design follows MongoDB best practices for document modeling.

Consolidation Strategy:

  • Films Collection: Embeds language, category, and actor information
  • Customers Collection: Embeds address and city data
  • Staff Collection: Embeds address and city information
  • Addresses Collection: Embeds city and country data
  • Stores Collection: Embeds address and city information
  • Rentals Collection: Embeds inventory and store data
  • Payments Collection: Embeds rental and inventory information
  • Inventories Collection: Embeds store and address data

Benefits: Reduced complexity, improved query performance, and better data locality for MongoDB operations.
